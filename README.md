## ðŸŒ¿ About the Game

**Anglocita Semesta: Restore the Nature** is a **2D Platformer & Adventure** game centered on **healing the environment rather than dominating it**.  
Players control a child exploring a post-ecological-collapse world, where nature exists as a wounded, living entity that must be restored through mindful interaction with the environment.

The game emphasizes **environmental storytelling**, **permanent world changes**, and **silence as a space for reflection**, avoiding combat and complex systems.

## ðŸ•¹ Gameplay Overview
- Exploration of damaged natural environments  
- Environmental challenges without combat  
- Collecting **water** and **organic waste**  
- Using fertilizer to neutralize aggressive plants  
- Unlocking new paths through **permanent environmental changes**  
- Progression conveyed through the world itself, not menus or statistics
